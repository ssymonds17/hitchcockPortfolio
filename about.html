About Me (Alfred)

Currently trying to make a living in film. Started my career in my home town of London before making the leap across the Atlantic in an attempt to break America.


Fond of all things thrilling or linked to suspense stemming from a childhood fear of the police that manifested from an experience of being locked up in the local station.


Had the opportunity to work with many actors who show real promise and I have my fingers crossed they each catch their big breaks soon.
